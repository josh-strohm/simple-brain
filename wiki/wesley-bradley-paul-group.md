# Wesley Bradley Paul Group

Business consulting firm founded by Omar Paul Strohm in Altoona, PA. Focuses on practical, no-fluff strategic advisory for companies facing operational plateaus. Emphasizes clarity, accountability, and measurable execution. Draws on founder's 20+ years in public-sector finance, labor relations, and municipal leadership (former City Manager of Altoona; current AVP Finance & CFO at Pennsylvania Highlands Community College).

**Philosophy:** "Sustainable success comes down to fundamentals. We work with business leaders to remove administrative friction, establish fiscal health, and leave their organizations stronger than they found them."

**Core offerings:**
- Strategic Advisory (prioritization, 1-page roadmaps, capital allocation, succession/M&A prep)
- Growth & Go-to-Market (funnel diagnostics, pricing optimization, pipeline audits, sales playbooks)
- Operational Clarity & Execution Support (process simplification, accountability frameworks, KPIs, scorecards)
- Ongoing Advisory Retainer (monthly steering calls, quarterly reviews, on-call crisis support)

**Engagement formats:** Diagnostic (2-4 weeks), Project-Based (3-6 months), Retainer (12 months).

**Differentiators:** 1-page playbooks, real-operations grounding, measurable progress via scorecards, low-risk changes first, confidential founder-led work.

**Website project:** Professional online presence with pages for Home, About, Services, Contact, Calendar (30-min strategy calls), Legal. Dark navy/grid visual system. Direct, jargon-free tone. Primary goal: qualified strategy call bookings.
