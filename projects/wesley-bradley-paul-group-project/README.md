## Overview
New business consulting firm focused on practical, no-fluff strategic advisory for growing companies at operational plateaus. Emphasizes clarity, accountability, and measurable execution. Leverages founder's 20+ years in public-sector finance, labor relations, and municipal leadership.

**Core belief:** "Practical business consulting for companies ready to move forward with clarity."

**Visual identity:** "The Strategic Grid" — structural balance, clear priorities, steady execution.

## Project Objectives
- Establish professional online presence for newly launched practice
- Communicate grounded, action-oriented consulting philosophy
- Drive qualified strategy call bookings via calendar integration
- Highlight founder credentials and Central PA roots
- Present modular engagement options (diagnostic, project-based, retainer)

## Site Structure
- **Home**: Hero messaging, core belief, common business thresholds (stalled growth, overwhelmed leadership, siloed alignment, fragmented execution, fractional executive gap), service overview, CTA to calendar
- **About**: Founder profile, advisory creed, philosophy pillars, why clients engage
- **Services**: Four core offerings with outputs + three engagement formats
  - Strategic Advisory (prioritization, roadmaps, capital allocation, succession/M&A)
  - Growth & Go-to-Market (funnel diagnostics, pricing, pipeline audits, sales playbooks)
  - Operational Clarity & Execution Support (process simplification, accountability frameworks, KPIs, scorecards)
  - Ongoing Advisory Retainer (monthly calls, quarterly reviews, crisis support)
  - Formats: Diagnostic (2-4 weeks), Project-Based (3-6 months), Retainer (12 months)
- **Contact**: Email/phone, form, commitment process
- **Calendar**: 30-minute strategy call booking
- **Legal**: Privacy Policy, Terms of Service

## Differentiators
